The Antimonopoly Club is a collective of lawyers and people who build products to tackle monopolies directly: by creating competition.

We commit to build and litigate towards systemic change to align value creation with profit. We believe that anyone who has a good idea and can build a good product should be able to enter the market without being blocked by large sellers or buyers. We stand for the right to repair, and the right to integrate proprietary hardware with other systems. We want people everywhere to be able to save money, generate less waste, and live easier lives.
